About

Ghetto school is a charity organisation which which supports young people through their talents so that they can have a better living.

History

Ghetto school was founded in 2015 by Dream Bouy, A ghetto youth artist who has thrived to come on the music scene and the inspirations come from there. He has hits like Abakyala and samanya where he featured Nina Roz.

Our Mission

To transform the lives of the ghetto youths through supporting their musical talents.

Objective

To make sure every ghetto youth has a chance to education and has support in their musical talents.
